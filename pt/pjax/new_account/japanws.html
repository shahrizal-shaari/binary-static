Cada transação de opção possui uma data e hora específica, conhecidas como a hora de julgamento.

If a customer holds a long position and the prediction goes wrong as of the judgment time, the invested principal amount will become zero value.

If a customer sells back a long option position before the determination time, then the sale price may be lower than the original purchase price, so there is a possibility of a net loss.

If a customer holds a short position and the prediction is incorrect as of the judgment time, the customer is required to pay the payout amount, and as that will be greater than the trade price that the customer originally received, this will cause a net loss.

A Binary.com obtém um retorno da diferença entre o valor total de todos os pagamentos de clientes e o valor total de todas as receitas de clientes.
